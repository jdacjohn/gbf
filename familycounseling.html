Family Counseling



The return to duty or a civilian role is challenging following a significant wound or injury. A major part of recovery and rehabilitation rests on the family, whether this is a wife and children, parents and siblings - or friends.

Green Berets, their wives and children always endure the stress of multiple deployments and the inherent secrecy that accompanies this lifestyle.




These routine demands are rendered far more complex by a severe wound or injury, family counseling can play a vital role in the recovery process. Both the Army as a whole, and Special Forces in particular recognize the importance and value

There are free and confidential resources available to Green Berets and their families. If you or a loved one seeks the services of a psychological professional, please visit: The Soldiers Project
