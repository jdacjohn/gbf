Pioneer Natural Resources


Pioneer Natural Resources is a company that supports our nation’s military in many ways -- by supporting our Reservists and National Guard employees when called to active duty, by initiating an aggressive hiring campaign of veterans and by supporting organizations such as the Green Beret Foundation and its efforts.

Our Special Forces soldiers are in harm’s way every day, whether in Afghanistan, Iraq or some other region. Those that have been wounded or need assistance deserve our support and we are honored to give it.

Scott D. Sheffield, Chairman and CEO

Green Beret Wife


The Green Beret Foundation has not only done immense work to get together a fund in my husband’s honor to help pay for supplements, meds, and other medical requirements not paid by insurance. But furthermore this great team of the GBF has shown tremendous support, taken active part in important life events whether it be a celebration or a need. Most of all, their mission to make sure no one is left behind, including families of soldiers, just shows one thing: the brother-and sisterhood in our community never goes away, even after injury or illness. Thank you GBF from the bottoms of our hearts - Your Team Baldwin

BG(R) Frank Toney | former Commanding General USASFC


The Green Beret Foundation serves in the true tradition of SF.  It gets right to the core of what is needed to best assist SF soldiers and their families in their time of need.

 

GBF understands that assistance doesn't have limits.  They understand the heart and soul of the SF Warrior, and they are committed to honoring their service and sacrifice to our country.  GBF cares and they show it. I'm proud to be a part of such an outstanding organization.

BG(R) Frank Toney
