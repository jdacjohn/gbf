We have been providing these services since 1999. One of our strengths is using a network of consultants and contacts with experienced and varied backgrounds. Juniors Bullet Pens

.................................................................................. Visit Them

Junior’s Bullet Pen Company is dedicated to one of our Nation's fallen heroes, SSG. Chris Falkel. He made weapons from just about anything he could find, and he always looked for a way to make a pen from bullets.

 

He made weapons from just about anything he could find, and he always looked for a way to make a pen from bullets.


Chris was killed-in-action on August 8, 2005 as he and the men of Special Forces ODA-316 engaged in a 54 hour battle with several hundred Taliban insurgents at Mari Ghar, Afghanistan. Dad learned to make Bullet Pens - and they are very special! Each pen is handmade from previously fired 7.62 military casings.

SSG. Matthew A. Pucino Memorial Foundation

.................................................................................. Visit Them

In honor of SSG. Matthew A. Pucino, 20th SFG(A), the Foundation continues the battle by creating a legacy of assistance for our wounded military heroes, their families and extended military families of the fallen. Their spirits will live in our hearts forever.

 

Matthew was assigned to Company B, 2nd Battalion, 20th Special Forces Group (Airborne).

On 23 November 2009, while on a combat patrol in the vicinity of Pashay Kala, Afghanistan, an improvised explosive device struck his all-terrain vehicle.

Special Forces Brotherhood MC


Mission: To provide a club for current and former members of the Special Forces Regiment, that love to ride and enjoy the company of those that ride. To provide an organization where like-minded warrior patriots can gather, express their views and opinions and enjoy each others company. To always honor our fallen brothers, their families and preserve the history of our brotherhood.
