Cooperation and Teamwork

USSOCOM Care Coalition


We work very closely with the USSOCOM Care Coalition to ensure that our casualties and their families’ needs are met with the utmost care and attention.

Their expertise, resources and experience across the full spectrum of Recovery-Rehabilitation-Reintegration is central to effectively addressing the needs of our wounded Special Forces.

For more information on the USSOCOM Care Coalition, click their image on the left.




Special Forces Association


The Special Forces Association is a fraternal organization of Green Berets, past and present, and consists of over 9000 members and 85 Chapters worldwide. The SFA has worked effectively for many years to promote and support the general welfare of the Special Forces community by perpetuating Special Forces traditions and camaraderie. This organization has long been instrumental in maintaining the fraternal bonds of thousands of Green Berets spanning all generations.

Although each has its unique mission and focus, the GBF and SFA work closely together in support of the Special Forces community. For more on the SFA including membership information and the location of a chapter near you, click on their image on the left.




O.A.S.I.S.


The OASIS Group provides Special Operations Veterans with a professional analysis of their claims in pursuit of Veterans Administration benefits. O.A.S.I.S. assists the Special Operations Warrior through its comprehensive understanding of the Veterans Administration processes and systems to ensure fair and thorough evaluation.

The GBF works with OASIS to provide briefings and assistance to Soldiers of the SF Groups by coordinating and facilitating OASIS team visits to the Group locations as needed. The GBF can also help coordinate OASIS support for our retired community. More information on OASIS can be found by clicking their image to the left.




No Greater Sacrifice


In fulfilling the GBF committment to Green Beret Families, No Greater Sacrifice works closely with us to enable our Scholarship Award twice a year for the children of wounded, ill or injured U.S. Army Special Forces soldiers.

No Greater Sacrifice is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the children of our nation’s fallen and wounded Service members by delivering scholarships and resources to improve their quality of life through the pursuit of higher education.
