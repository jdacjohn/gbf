GBF Financials and Documents

 

 

 

2011 was the second year of operations for the Green Beret Foundation.  Eighty-nine cents of every dollar spent in 2011 went to support the Green Beret Foundation’s mission.   The GBF’s Board and Staff take great pride in our Programs and Services percentage for both our first and second years of operations. 


In true Green Beret fashion and with the loyal support of our Community and supporters, the GBF was able to force multiply by using a smaller number of people and resources to achieve results that would  be expected from a larger organization and resource pool.  We have been able to keep our fundraising and administrative costs at 11% combined ensuring that we are optimizing our support to our Green Beret Community.
